About me

My name is Juan Vivas but you can call me Juanito. I am based in Long Beach Island, New Jersey, but I'm from Colombia. I have been doing photography for more than six years. I am an English and French teacher, and Spanish is my native language. I always do my very best when I take photos, and just like in teaching, I do it with love and passion.
